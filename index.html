Alight Motion Introduction

The Inside of the Application

When you first open Alight motion you will see 5 tabs below the 2 on the left are called "Home" and "Tutorials" the one in the middle is for creating projects which is a space to create videos and effects etc...The 2 on the right are "Projects" and "Elements"...

  • Home Button: When the application is first open you will be in the home tab which shows new updates and features
  • Tutorials: Teaches you how to use Alight Motion and it's many Feauters step by step guide
  • Projects Button: By pressing the big button + sign in the bottom, it will create new a project platform
  • Regular Project Button: Will show all your created projects
  • Elements Button: Create your own Elements or Use others
